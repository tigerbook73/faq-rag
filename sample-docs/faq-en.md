# FAQ: Company Leave Policy

## Q: How many vacation days do employees get per year?

Employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year for their first 3 years. After 3 years, they receive 20 days per year. After 10 years of service, employees are entitled to 25 days per year.

## Q: How do I apply for leave?

Submit a leave request through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance for regular leave. For emergency leave, notify your direct manager immediately and follow up with an HR form within 2 business days.

## Q: What is the sick leave policy?

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days. Unused sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

## Q: Can I carry over unused vacation days?

Yes, up to 5 unused vacation days can be carried over to the next calendar year. Any remaining unused days beyond the 5-day limit will be forfeited.

## Q: What is the parental leave policy?

Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.
