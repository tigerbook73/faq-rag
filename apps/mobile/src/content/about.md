## About FAQ RAG

A single-user FAQ question-answering system.

Upload documents in Chinese or English, ask questions in either language, and get streamed answers with cited source chunks.

## Key Features

- Upload and manage your knowledge base (PDF / DOCX / Markdown / TXT)
- Multilingual Q&A (English / Chinese) with real-time streamed answers
- Retrieval with semantic chunking, vector search, and query expansion (HyDE)
